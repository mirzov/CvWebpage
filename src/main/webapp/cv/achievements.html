Oleg Mirzov's achievements

Apart from the publications (including the bachelor, master, licentiate and PhD theses), there are some specific achievements worth mentioning.

Work achievements
  • The website of Chemical Physics department (created a content management system based on the Protégé ontology editor and wrote the necessary XSLT style sheets for it).
  • Served as the main driving force behind creation of the new technique - 2D polarisation single-molecule spectroscopy (produced most of the key ideas and wrote all the software; the experimental part was driven by Ivan Scheblykin with help from Daniel Thomsson).

  • First-author cover article publication:

    Phys.Chem.Chem.Phys., 2006, 8, 5569
  • Wrote a package of software that is crucial for the work of the group.
  • Succeeded in designing and fabricating an organic light-emitting diode (OLED) from a novel lanthanide complex (August 2002, FIAN, Moscow, Russia):

    ITO/PEDOT:PSS/PVK/Tb(Sal)3(TPPO)2/Al OLED
